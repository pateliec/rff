Standard Luggage

You can opt to have your luggage delivered to your Rottnest Island accommodation on your behalf. Each item for delivery needs to be labelled, however there is no charge or label required for carry-on luggage.

$3.00/item

Luggage & Accommodation Details

Please note there is a weight limit of 22kg per item of luggage for delivery. Items weighing more than this will not be delivered to your accommodation, and you will need to collect them from the Rottnest Island Visitor Centre.

On the day of departure, you will need to collect your luggage labels from our office at Hillarys Boat Harbour. To assist in transporting your luggage to your accommodation, please ensure that:

  1. All items are labelled.
  2. Valuables and medication are stored in your carry-on luggage (not checked for delivery).

Please remember that craypots, golf clubs/buggies, fishing rods, diving equipment, surfboards and bikes will NOT be delivered to your accommodation. You will need to collect these directly from the ferry upon your arrival to Rottnest Island."

Please add your accommodation details to ensure that your luggage is delivered to your room.
